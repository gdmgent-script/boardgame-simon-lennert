Rules/explanation

These are the rules and the explanations of the game

---
Game Rules: Emergency Response Challenge!

Objective:  
Collect the most  Cards by giving the best explanation for how to handle emergency scenarios.

Setup:  
- A slot machine (simulated)  
- Three card decks:  
  - Red Action Cards (emergency scenarios)  
  - Blue Obstacle Cards (complications)  
  - Black Persona Cards (personal conditions)  
- A timer (phone, hourglass, etc.)

Gameplay:

1. Spin the Slot Machine  
   - 3 different symbols: Draw 1 card  
   - 2 identical symbols: Draw 2 cards  
   - 3 identical symbols: Draw 3 cards

2. Draw Cards in Order:  
   - 1st card (Red) – Action Card (e.g., Heart Attack)  
   - 2nd card (Blue) – Obstacle Card (e.g., traffic jam)  
   - 3rd card (Black) – Persona Card (e.g., person has a pacemaker)

3. Discussion Phase:  
   - Players discuss the best way to handle the situation described by the cards.  
   - Set a timer for the discussion (duration is up to you).

4. Judging:  
   - After time runs out, all players vote on who gave the best explanation.  
   - The winner keeps Cards.

5. End of Game:  
   - The game ends when all red Action Cards have been used.  
   - The player with the most cards wins!

6. Solutions:  
   - Scan the QR code to view possible solutions.  
   - Look for the section labeled “Solutions” above the code.
